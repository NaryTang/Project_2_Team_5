Homelessness in the United States (2010 - 2017)

With rising housing prices, increasing gentrification of neighborhoods, and rent disrupting apps like Airbnb, Homelessness has become one of the biggest issues in the us. We can see the issue alarmed the federal government through their increase of HUD’s budget by 29 billion dollars in 2017.

For our project, we’d like to explore how the homeless population has changed over the past decade and what factors may impact homelessness trends in the United States.

Initial finding: Contrary to HUD's budget increase in 2017 (and our anecodtal experience in California), nationwide, our homeless population has actually decreased from 2010 - 2017.
